Zwolle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Zwolle is a cycling workout app. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you create stays on your device.

Data stored on your device

Zwolle may store workout files and settings locally on your device. This data is not accessible to us and is never uploaded anywhere.

Bluetooth

Zwolle uses Bluetooth Low Energy to communicate with cycling sensors and trainers. Bluetooth data is processed locally on your device and is not recorded or transmitted.

Location

Zwolle requests location permission solely because it is required by Android to scan for Bluetooth devices. The app does not access, record, or transmit your location.

Third-party services

Zwolle does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other third-party services that collect data.

Changes to this policy

If future versions of Zwolle introduce data collection, this policy will be updated accordingly before those features are released.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue at github.com/NetworkAndSoftware/legal.
